About Us

Our Mission

ThinkLock turns everyday screen time into something sharper, faster, and more rewarding. Built around the idea that learning should feel like a game—not a chore—we combine microlearning with competition, streaks, and real-time progress to make critical thinking addictive. Whether you've got seconds or minutes, every interaction is designed to challenge your mind and build momentum. Our mission is simple: help people train their thinking the same way they train anything else—consistently, competitively, and with purpose.
