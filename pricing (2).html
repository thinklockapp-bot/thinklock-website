FAQ

Common questions

What's the difference between Basic and Pro?+
Basic gives you 3 rounds per game every day with no ads and streak shields. Pro unlocks unlimited daily play, deep performance stats, and instant access to every new game we launch.
Can I switch between Monthly and Annual?+
Yes — you can upgrade or downgrade your billing cycle anytime from your account settings. Annual billing saves you up to 44%.
Is there a free trial?+
The app is free to download with limited access. Paid plans unlock the full experience instantly with no trial period needed.
Can I cancel anytime?+
Absolutely. Cancel from the App Store or your account settings at any time. You'll keep access until the end of your current billing period.
What are streak shields?+
Streak shields protect your daily streak if you miss a day. They automatically activate so you never lose your chain — even when life gets in the way.
