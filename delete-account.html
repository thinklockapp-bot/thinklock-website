Permanent Action

Delete Account

⚠️ IMPORTANT

Deleting your account will permanently remove all your game progress, statistics, friends, and achievements. This action cannot be undone.

What Gets Deleted

• All game scores and statistics
• AI level progress and achievements
• Friend connections and leaderboard rankings
• Profile information and preferences
• Account login credentials

How to Delete Your Account

1
Open the ThinkLock app
2
Go to Settings
3
Select Delete Account
4
Select YES
5
This cannot be undone — be certain you want to delete your account and all data associated with your account, stats, achievements, etc. before pressing YES
