Privacy Policy

Summary

ThinkLock is a safe, educational app that stores all data locally on your device. We do not collect personal information, especially from children under 13.

Information We Collect

• Game progress and scores (stored locally only)
• Performance analytics for adaptive learning
• User preferences and settings
• Usage statistics for app improvement

Information We Do Not Collect

• Personal identifying information
• Location data
• Camera or microphone access
• Contact lists
• Financial information
• Email addresses

COPPA Compliance

• Fully compliant with Children's Online Privacy Protection Act
• No personal information collected from children under 13
• All data stored locally on device
• No account creation required
• Parents maintain full control

Data Storage

• All information stored locally on your device
• No cloud storage or external servers
• Delete data anytime by uninstalling app
• Industry-standard encryption

Parental Rights

• Review all game progress and statistics
• Delete data by uninstalling app
• Set device-level parental controls
• Contact us with privacy questions

Contact

Email: privacy@thinklock.app
Subject: "Privacy Policy Question"
Response time: Within 48 hours

ThinkLock is committed to protecting children's privacy while providing valuable educational experiences.
